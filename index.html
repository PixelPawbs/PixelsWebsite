Games

I don't really play games. Like, at all. I play Minecraft sometimes, but it's hard to just make a world and... play. I just don't like games. I can sit down and play for like, 30 minutes, but that's it. I never really feel like playing it again, which really sucks when you waste your money on a game everyone else loves, then you play it, and you don't like it.

get major FOMO when it comes to games too. Like with Mario Kart World. I wanted to get a Switch 2 just so I could play it. But then, I took a step back and thought "If I get this, would I play it?". Of course the answer was yes, or so I thought. As I thought about it more, I didn't really see much point in buying the console if I wasn't ever going to play the games.

I don't have any friends, like, at all. I have no one else to play it with. All I would be doing is doing the grand prix... For what? What satisfaction would that give? I beat a bunch of computers, yay. And, if the AI is anything like Mario Kart 8 Deluxe, it's not gonna be fun. I'll always be in 1st, 2nd, or 3rd, or just be losing the whole time. It won't be fun.

I've heard there's a free-play, exploration-type mode and.... like... cool i guess. I don't understand why you would do that? Why would you want to just roam around a giant map?
